About Me

I’m Ashutosh Takpire, a passionate dance choreographer and theatre artist. I’ve had the opportunity to participate in the reality show *MAD (Maharashtracha Assal Dancer)*, where I reached the Top 10. Over the years, I’ve choreographed numerous songs and have extensive teaching experience, conducting workshops and classes.
Currently, I serve as a choreographer at PCCOE Art Circle, a role I’ve proudly held for the last two years. I’ve also had the privilege of winning several awards, including prizes in the prestigious Firodia Karandak for physical theatre and best dance team.
